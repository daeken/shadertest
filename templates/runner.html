Warning: This page will be running shaders submitted by arbitrary users. It could crash your browser, and your user-agent and GPU/WebGL information will be seen by anyone getting information from you. Just keep this in mind.

Before you can start Shadertest for the first time, you have to tell us a bit about your GPU.
If you're using Chrome, go to chrome://gpu and copy the "GL_RENDERER" line from the "Driver Information" table here.
If you're using Firefox, go to about:support and copy the "WebGL Renderer" line from the "Graphics" table here.
